# ── Scope note ───────────────────────────────────────────
# Antivirals are deliberately EXCLUDED. This dictionary supports a bacterial
# culture & sensitivity workflow; viruses are not isolated on routine bacterial
# media, so an antiviral name here would imply a capability the lab does not
# have. Antifungals are included because yeasts DO grow on routine culture
# (Candida in urine, sputum and wound swabs) and get reported from this bench.
